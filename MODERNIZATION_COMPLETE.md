## How to Add Your Images (Quick Guide)

### Easy Self-Hosted Method:

1. Go to **Unsplash.com** (free images)
2. Search for "bracelet" or "jewelry"
3. Right-click image → "Copy image address"
4. Open: `src/components/HeroCarousel.tsx`
5. Replace the `image:` URLs with your copied URLs
6. Save and refresh browser
7. Done!

Example:
```typescript
// Line 19 in HeroCarousel.tsx
image: 'https://YOUR-IMAGE-URL-HERE',
```
